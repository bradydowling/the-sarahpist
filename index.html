About Me

As a licensed clinical psychologist at Massachusetts General Hospital and instructor at Harvard Medical School, I bring deep expertise in understanding therapeutic approaches and matching clients with the right care. My extensive experience in cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), exposure therapy (ERP), and other evidence-based treatments, combined with my research in digital mental health interventions, gives me unique insight into what makes therapy truly effective.

Through my work supervising clinicians and developing innovative mental health solutions, I've gained a comprehensive understanding of different therapeutic styles and approaches. This expertise allows me to thoughtfully match you with a therapist whose methods and personality will best serve your specific needs.

My Service

I offer a personalized therapist matching service that takes the stress out of finding the right mental health professional. Through careful consideration of your specific needs, preferences, and circumstances, I connect you with therapists who are the best fit for your journey.
